What we do

A full-service crew, not a chainsaw and a truck

From a single dead pine to a whole lot cleared, we bring the right equipment for the job — and the license and insurance to do it safely.

01

Tree Removal & Dangerous Takedowns

The leaning oak over your roof. The dead pine near the power line. We rig, section, and lower big trees in tight spots where dropping them whole isn't an option — cleanly, with the yard intact.

  • Hazardous / leaning trees
  • Over-structure removals
  • Full lot clearing
Crane & rigging work
02

Crane-Assisted Lifts

Some trees are too big, too dead, or too boxed-in to climb safely. Our crane lifts each section out clean over the house and drops it in the drop zone — faster, safer, and far easier on your property than the old way.

  • High-ticket / complex jobs
  • Backyard & over-house lifts
  • Minimal ground impact
Heavy equipment
03

Stump Grinding

We don't leave you staring at a stump. We grind it below grade — roots and flare — so you can sod, plant, or just mow right over it. Chips hauled or left as mulch, your call.

  • Ground below grade
  • Chips hauled or mulched
  • Any size, any spot
Below-grade grinding
04

Storm Response & Cleanup

Louisiana weather doesn't wait, and neither do we. After a storm we clear downed trees off homes, driveways, and fences, then haul it all off — branches, leaves, wood, and chips — so your yard looks like nothing happened.

  • Emergency removals
  • Debris haul-off
  • Trimming & pruning
